The Library

The WWU Library / Photos by Julian Stefanzick

The WWU Library, located on the southern corner of red square, contains equal parts knowledge and beauty. Rows of books and rows of computers make up this central hub of student productivity. The larget "quiet study room" upstairs is especially popular for those looking to lock into their work.
