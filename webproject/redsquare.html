Red Square

WWU Red Square / Photos by Adrian Metzelaar

Red Square is one of the primary hubs for alumni at WWU, located near the library and many other academic buildings in North campus. Many fairs throughout the year take place here, as well as the occasional stand set-up by anyone who wants to communicate a message.
