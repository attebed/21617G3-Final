Performing Arts Center

Photo description/credits.

This page is dedicated to the beauty of the Performing Arts Center. The building hosts several concert halls, as well as practice and class rooms. Adjacent to the building is one of the most beautiful views on campus. All quarters of the year students may be found enjoying the scenery that is Bellingham Bay.
